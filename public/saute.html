Overview

SAUTÉ is an AI-powered meal planning platform that turns the daily question of "what's for dinner?" into a seamless, personalized experience. It combines intelligent recipe matching, pantry management, leftover threading, and one-tap grocery checkout to make nutritious, delicious meals accessible to everyone.

From swipe-based recipe discovery to receipt scanning that builds your digital pantry in seconds, every feature was designed around a single goal: removing friction from the way people eat.

The Problem

Meal planning is broken. The average household spends 40+ minutes a day deciding what to eat, wastes ~30% of groceries purchased, and overspends on duplicate ingredients across disconnected shopping trips. Existing solutions are either recipe databases that ignore your pantry, or rigid meal kits that remove all choice.

SAUTÉ sits in between: intelligent enough to know what's in your fridge, flexible enough to let you cook what you want, and integrated enough to handle the shopping.

Core Features

01
Swipe-Based Discovery

Every recipe is scored against your pantry, goals, and taste profile. Swipe right to plan, left for alternatives. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner planned in under a minute.

02
Leftover Threading

SAUTÉ tracks ingredients across your entire week. Monday's salmon automatically becomes Wednesday's grain bowl. Users reduced food waste by up to 60% in testing.

03
Receipt Scanning

Snap a grocery receipt and Vision AI reads every item, building a digital pantry with quantities and expiration estimates. Works with DoorDash and Instacart orders too.

04
Smart Cart & Checkout

Missing ingredients are auto-added to a unified cart, grouped by recipe with exact prices. Smart bundling means one bottle of sesame oil covers six recipes. Checkout via DoorDash for same-day delivery.

05
Taste Profiling

A living map of your flavor preferences — savory, bright, spicy, rich, sweet — that learns from every recipe you cook and every dish you skip. After just 5 meals, recommendations adapt to you.

06
Calendar Sync

Planned meals sync directly with Google Calendar as events with prep time and recipe links, so you never forget what's for dinner.

Design Direction

SAUTÉ's interface draws from the visual language of premium food photography and editorial design — warm burgundy and sage tones, generous whitespace, and typography that balances approachability with sophistication (Cormorant Garamond for headings, Inter for body). The entire experience is presented within an iPhone-frame mockup, emphasizing mobile-first design.

The onboarding flow guides users through name, dietary preferences, cooking time availability, household size, and an optional receipt upload — building a complete profile in under 90 seconds. From there, the main interface uses a tab-based navigation: Tonight, Week, Explore, Pantry, You, and Cart.

Technical Architecture

The front-end is built with React 18 and Vite for near-instant HMR during development, styled with Tailwind CSS for rapid iteration. The swipe-based recipe cards use custom touch event handling with velocity-based threshold detection for natural gesture interaction.

Pantry management leverages Vision AI for receipt OCR, automatically extracting items, quantities, and prices from photographed receipts. The recipe matching engine scores each dish against three dimensions: pantry fit, personal goals (recovery, performance, comfort), and taste profile affinity.

Leftover threading uses a dependency graph between recipes — tracking which ingredients carry forward and suggesting meals that consume leftovers before they expire. The smart cart deduplicates ingredients across the week's meal plan and integrates directly with DoorDash for one-tap grocery delivery.
