Personas & Storyboarding

User research case study — Mail Room Kiosk at Brown University

UX Research Personas Storyboarding
+

Overview

Conducted user research on the Brown University Mail Room kiosk interface. Created detailed personas based on observation and contextual inquiry, then developed storyboards to illustrate pain points and opportunities in the mail retrieval experience.

Approach

Observed 20+ users interacting with the kiosk, conducted brief interviews, synthesized findings into two distinct personas, and created a comprehensive storyboard mapping the end-to-end journey.
